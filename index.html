About Us

BreakingTheCycle is a youth-led nonprofit organization focused on raising awareness about Polycystic Ovarian Syndrome (PCOS) among young women.

PCOS is a hormonal condition that affects many women and even their journey into motherhood. However, it is commonly misunderstood, which is why our mission is to help women better understand PCOS, its impact, and provide resources for managing the condition.

We are based in Chandler, Arizona, but we are always expanding to reach more and more people!
